# Tier 1 — Adaptive differentiated Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.



Adaptive differentiated apps use the large screen and foldable form factors to
their full potential. Differentiated apps offer a premium user experience that's
productive and delightful.

![Depiction of the three quality tiers as layers stacked vertically with the top tier highlighted.](/static/images/docs/quality-guidelines/tier-1/tier_1_header.png)

Because Tier 1 apps are highly differentiated, the Tier 1 guidelines are
organized by app *experiences*—the type of app you're creating or the type
of device you're targeting.

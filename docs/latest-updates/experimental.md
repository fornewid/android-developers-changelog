### Gemini Nano experimental access!

October 1, 2024Available in Google AI Edge SDKGemini Nano is Google's most efficient AI model built for on-device tasks on Android. Try Gemini Nano today to experiment with text-to-text prompts on Pixel 9 devices. Watch for more devices and modalities in the future!

### Desktop windowing on Android tablets

SEPTEMBER 12, 2024Available in Android 15 QPR1Get your apps ready for desktop windowing! With this feature, users can run multiple apps simultaneously in individually resizable windows. Now in Developer Preview to test your apps on Android 15 QPR1 Beta 2 or later.

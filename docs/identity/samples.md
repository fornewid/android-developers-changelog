### [SMS Verify App](https://github.com/android/identity-samples/tree/main/SmsVerification)

This sample demonstrates the use of Play Service's SMS Verification APIs to get access to specially-tagged text messages (the tags associate the message with the APK) without needing full SMS retrieval permission (SMS\_READ).

[View on GitHub](https://github.com/android/identity-samples/tree/main/SmsVerification)

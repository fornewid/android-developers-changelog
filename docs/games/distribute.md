# Google Play Games

[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/distribute/googleplay/games-play-hero.svg)](http://developer.android.com/games/pgs/overview)  

### [Games on Google Play](http://developer.android.com/games/pgs/overview)

Make the most of Google Play to test, distribute, optimize, and monetize your game.  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/games/pgs/overview)[Go to Play Console](http://play.google.com/console)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/games/distribute/google-play-libraries.svg)  

## Integrate Play features

The Play libraries are your app's runtime interface with the Google Play Store.  
[Learn More](http://developer.android.com/guide/playcore)  

### [Sell digital products and content](http://developer.android.com/google/play/billing)

Use Google Play's billing system to sell consumable or non-consumable one-time products as well as subscriptions.  

### [Distribute game assets](http://developer.android.com/guide/playcore/asset-delivery)

Play Asset Delivery offers flexible delivery modes, auto-updates, compression, and delta patching served by Google Play at no cost to you.  

### [Sign-in and cloud saves](https://developers.android.com/games/pgs/overview)

Google Play Games Services enables low-friction cross device and instant play by saving game data on Google's infrastructure. It includes achievements, leaderboards, friends, and more.  

### [In-app updates](http://developer.android.com/guide/playcore/in-app-updates)

Prompt active users to update your game with both flexible and immediate updates.  

### [In-app reviews](http://developer.android.com/guide/playcore/in-app-review)

Prompt users to submit Play Store ratings and reviews without the inconvenience of leaving your game.![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/games/distribute/games-play-launch.svg)  

## Launch your game

Use the Google Play Console to launch your game to a global audience.  
[Learn More](https://play.google.com/console/about/)  

### [Build a high-quality game](https://play.google.com/console/about/build-a-high-quality-app-or-game/)

Guidelines to help improve content, user experience, technical performance, and more.  

### [Testing tracks](http://developer.android.com/distribute/best-practices/launch/test-tracks)

Use testing tracks to gather feedback and improve your game.  

### [Pre-registration](https://play.google.com/console/about/pre-registration/)

Create excitement and awareness of your game before it launches.  

### [Release with confidence](https://play.google.com/console/about/releasewithconfidence/)

Understand policies, build your store listing, troubleshoot before launching, and more.  

### [Device compatibility](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/7353455)

View the catalog of available devices and exclude ones that can't offer a good experience.

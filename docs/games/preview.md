### Developer preview programs for game developers

Apply to join open betas and developer preview programs from Android and Google Play and shape the development of these cutting edge features with your feedback. Acceptance into each program is subject to game titles meeting program requirements. For more information on building games for Android, see the main [**Games page**](/games).

### [Android GPU Inspector](https://developer.android.com/agi)

Android GPU Inspector is a GPU profiling tool. You can take traces of your games and find interesting performance insights to help you make optimization decisions.

[Learn more](https://developer.android.com/agi)

[![](https://developer.android.com/static/games/preview/images/googleplaynew.png)](https://developer.android.com/google/play/integrity/device-recall)

Public beta

### [Device recall](https://developer.android.com/google/play/integrity/device-recall)

Device recall in the Play Integrity API lets you detect repeat abuse even after your game is reinstalled or the device is factory reset. You can customize what to recall, such as reidentifying devices being reused to create new accounts after they were banned for cheating.

[Learn more](https://developer.android.com/google/play/integrity/device-recall)

### [ADB Incremental](https://developer.android.com/about/versions/11/features#incremental)

With ADB Incremental in Android 11, installing large APKs (2GB+) from your development computer to an Android 11 device is up to 10x faster.

[Learn more](https://developer.android.com/about/versions/11/features#incremental)

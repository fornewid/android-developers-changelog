# Adaptive apps case studies

## Increase your user base...
Optimize for all form factors

Engineering teams around the globe have improved their customer metrics by building adaptive experiences for all screens where your mobile app runs. Learn from the developers themselves about the experiences they built, what they implemented, and why this is important to their businesses.  
[Get inspired](http://developer.android.com/large-screens/gallery)[Start building](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive)

### Build adaptive layouts for 500M+ active large screen users

Target all display sizes effortlessly with layouts that dynamically adapt to fill available space and take advantage of new device features.[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/layout.svg)](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-different-display-sizes)  

### [Scale across screen sizes](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-different-display-sizes)

Android devices come in all shapes and sizes. Discover APIs that help you manage configuration changes and layout resizing.  
[Get started](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-different-display-sizes)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/responsive-layout.svg)](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-multi-window-mode)  

### [Support multitasking](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-multi-window-mode)

On large screens, users often have more than one application visible. Learn about multitasking and multi-window mode and how to support features users love like picture-in-picture and drag and drop.  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-multi-window-mode)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/foldables.svg)](http://developer.android.com/quality-guidelines/large-screen-app-quality#large_screen_ready_tests)  

### [Evaluate your application](http://developer.android.com/quality-guidelines/large-screen-app-quality#large_screen_ready_tests)

Apps must satisfy criteria across several quality tiers to ensure that the apps fully support large screens. Review the compatibility tests and their corresponding checklists to determine whether your app is ready for large screens.  
[Review the tests](http://developer.android.com/quality-guidelines/large-screen-app-quality#large_screen_ready_tests)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/learning-resources.svg)](https://material.io/blog/material-design-for-large-screens)  

### [Design for all screens](https://material.io/blog/material-design-for-large-screens)

Material Design has guidance to help you optimize your app with common adaptive layouts and approaches to responsive grids. Explore the latest guidance to find out how you can design and build for more devices.  
[Read more](https://material.io/blog/material-design-for-large-screens)

Optimizing for all screens means meeting your users where they are with experiences that inspire.

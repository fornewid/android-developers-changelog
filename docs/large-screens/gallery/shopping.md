# Shopping

![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/icons/shopping.svg)  

### Shopping

Satisfy your customer's needs by showing the right merchandise at the right time, offering a combination of focus and exploration that adapts to a device's screen size.Adaptive apps show product highlights to shoppers, along with options to see specifications and more details in a smooth navigation experience.  
List-detail

### Product specs

Speed up buying decisions with a scrollable list of products shoppers can select from to see product descriptions, specifications, manufacturer data, star ratings---all on one screen for convenient viewing that rings up sales quickly.  
[Learn about list-detail design](https://material.io/foundations/layout/canonical-layouts/list-detail)[Implement a list-detail layout](https://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/large-screen-canonical-layouts#list-detail)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/shopping/Catalogs-and-promotions.png)  
Feed

### Catalogs and promotions

Create enticing product collections in a large display space that enables detailed imagery and compelling copy for motivated buying. Promote products by using outsized dimensions and prominent positioning to focus attention and generate buying interest.  
[Learn about feed design](https://material.io/foundations/layout/canonical-layouts/feed)[Implement a feed layout](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/large-screen-canonical-layouts#feed)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/shopping/Supporting-the-sale.png)  
Supporting pane

### Supporting the sale

Turn shopping into buying with assistive features like product filters for targeted searches or a checklist of product options for a subtle upsell. Leverage large screen space to keep assistance readily accessible in a supporting pane while still leaving plenty of room for product viewing.  
[Learn about supporting pane design](https://material.io/foundations/layout/canonical-layouts/supporting-pane)[Implement a supporting pane layout](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/large-screen-canonical-layouts#supporting_pane)

## Differentiated experiences

Distinctive features and capabilities not possible on small screen devices.![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/shopping/Multi-window.png)  

### Multi-window

Expedite informed, confident buying decisions by enabling shoppers to view products in one window while researching product ratings, reviews, and reports in another.  
[See "Support multi-window mode"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-multi-window-mode)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/shopping/Drag-and-drop.png)  

### Drag and drop

Make purchasing quick and easy with a drag-and-drop shopping cart. Capture prospective purchases in a drag-and-drop wish list. Enable shoppers to share bargains and buys with family and friends using drag and drop from shopping apps to chat or email apps in multi-window mode.  
[See "Enable drag and drop"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/views/touch-and-input/drag-drop)

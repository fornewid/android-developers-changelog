# Creativity

![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/icons/creativity.svg)  

### Creativity

Help users make the most of the space that's available on their device, offering a variety of options to capture big ideas and fine details.Imagine. Tablet as creative canvas. Stylus as brush and pen, turning concepts into creations, possibilities into reality, in a large screen design space made for more imagining.

## Stylus features

[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/creativity/low-latency.png)](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#low_latency)  

### [Low latency](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#low_latency)

Low latency input turns the stylus into a precision tool, providing a natural, organic writing or drawing experience.  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#low_latency)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/creativity/hover.png)](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#hover)  

### [Hover](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#hover)

Stylus hover enables contextual information and feedback, such as tooltips, UI highlighting, and messaging.  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#hover)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/creativity/palm-rejection.png)](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#palm_rejection)  

### [Palm rejection](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#palm_rejection)

Palm detection and rejection eliminate inadvertent screen touches for comfortable, natural stylus control.  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#palm_rejection)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/creativity/tilt-pressure.png)](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#pressure)  

### [Tilt and pressure](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#pressure)

Tilt and pressure support enable advanced input capabilities, such as variation in stroke width, color saturation, or touch intensity.  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/touch-input/stylus-input/advanced-stylus-features#pressure)

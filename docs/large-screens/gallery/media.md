### Media

Create a big-screen media experience that adapts.Each screen size provides unique benefits for viewing feature films or short-form content. Give your users a better viewing experience across all screen sizes, enhancing the experience with playlists and photos. Make all kinds of media more discoverable and more enjoyable.

## Differentiated experiences

Distinctive features and capabilities not possible on small screen devices.![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/media/tabletop.png)  

### Tabletop

Let users lean back and enjoy their favorite content with tabletop posture on foldables. Place playback media above the fold, controls and supplementary content below, for a hands-free viewing or listening experience.  
[Learn about foldable device design](https://m3.material.io/foundations/layout/applying-layout/window-size-classes)[See "Make your app fold aware"](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/make-apps-fold-aware#tabletop_mode)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/media/picture-in-picture.png)  

### Picture-in-picture

Bring the small screen to the big screen so users can watch and work, learn and do at the same time.  
[See "Picture-in-picture (PIP) support"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/views/picture-in-picture)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/media/multi-window.png)  

### Multi-window

Let users multitask with two apps side by side to browse a collection of movies while checking out casts and characters, or listen to music while searching for covers or the latest music news and reviews.  
[See "Support multi-window mode"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-multi-window-mode)

# Productivity

![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/icons/productivity.svg)  

### Productivity

Your phone is your go-to tool for daily tasks. For tasks big and small, go for the extended capabilities of large screens.Provide a place for users to be productive, regardless of their device's screen size. Focus attention on the details that matter, while offering smooth navigation and logical layouts to show supporting information.  
Feed

### File browsing and filtering

Display handfuls of thumbnails to enable users to filter and find documents, images, spreadsheets---any kind of file---faster.  
[Learn about feed layout design](https://material.io/foundations/layout/canonical-layouts/feed)[Implement a feed layout](https://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/large-screen-canonical-layouts#feed)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/productivity/Items-and-overviews.png)  
List-detail

### Items and overviews

Show more details about list details on another screen, or adjacent to the list, depending on the screen size.  
[Learn about list-detail design](https://material.io/foundations/layout/canonical-layouts/list-detail)[Implement a list-detail layout](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/large-screen-canonical-layouts#list-detail)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/productivity/Tool-panels-in-context.png)  
Supporting pane

### Tools and settings

Give users access to more tools, larger palettes, more settings without obscuring content or hindering workflow.  
[Learn about supporting pane design](https://material.io/foundations/layout/canonical-layouts/supporting-pane)[Implement a supporting pane layout](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/large-screen-canonical-layouts#supporting_pane)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/productivity/Reviews-and-comments.png)  
Supporting pane

### Reviews and comments

Enable reviews and comments on documents directly, on the same screen without obstructing the document or disturbing the context.  
[Learn about supporting pane design](https://material.io/foundations/layout/canonical-layouts/supporting-pane)[Implement a supporting pane layout](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/large-screen-canonical-layouts#supporting_pane)

## Differentiated experiences

Distinctive features and capabilities not possible on small screen devices.![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/productivity/drag-and-drop.png)  

### Drag and drop

Enable intuitive content sharing in a large, unconfined work space that makes drag and drop natural, comfortable, simple---between widespread views in the same app or, in multi-window mode, between apps.  
[See "Enable drag and drop"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/views/touch-and-input/drag-drop)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/productivity/tabletop.png)  

### Tabletop

Design tabletop layouts that enable users to drop what they're doing and focus on what's important---a colleague's presentation, a video conference call---while still having easy access to other content and app controls hands free.  
[Learn about designing for foldable devices](https://m3.material.io/foundations/layout/applying-layout/window-size-classes)[See "Make your app fold aware"](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/make-apps-fold-aware#tabletop_mode)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/productivity/multi-window.png)  

### Multi-window

Get apps working together side by side in multi-window mode, with space for note-taking while reading an article or watching a video.  
[See "Support multi-window mode"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/compose/layouts/adaptive/support-multi-window-mode)[See "Stylus overview"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/views/touch-and-input/stylus)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/productivity/keyboard-mouse-trackpad.png)  

### Keyboard, mouse, and trackpad

Accelerate productivity with keyboard, mouse, and trackpad support for speedy typing, quick keyboard shortcuts, and more nimble UI interaction and control.  
[See "Input compatibility on large screens"](http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/large-screens/input-compatibility-large-screens)[See "Handle keyboard actions"](http://developer.android.com/develop/ui/views/touch-and-input/keyboard-input/commands)

### Chrome's multitasking usage increases 18x on large screens

Chrome users requested more productivity features on tablets, foldables, and phones to better match the capabilities of Chrome on desktop. The Chrome team responded by building multitasking capabilities into the Chrome browser.

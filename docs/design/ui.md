## Design for Android

Design beautiful and modern Android apps that meet your user where they are, whether browsing their phone, reading on their tablet, glancing at their wrist, working on a ChromeOS device, or watching TV.

## Design for mobile

Create a beautiful and usable modern app design using Android themes, components, and adaptive layouts.  
[Explore mobile design →](http://developer.android.com/design/ui/mobile)

## Adapt to multiple form factors

Create an immersive and engaging user experience with layouts that resize and reconfigure to optimize presentation, interactivity, and usability.  
[Explore adaptive design →](http://developer.android.com/design/ui/large-screens)[Get inspired by UX designs for a variety of screens →](http://developer.android.com/large-screens/gallery)

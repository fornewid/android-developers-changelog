# Components Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.



The following components are used in multiple templates. Here, you'll find
guidance on what the component is and how to apply it.

* [Action strip](/design/ui/cars/guides/components/action-strip): Put secondary or tertiary actions a tap away.
* [Map action strip](/design/ui/cars/guides/components/map-action-strip): Give users access to map interactivity features.
* [Button](/design/ui/cars/guides/components/button): Provide access to actions that drivers may need to take.
* [Floating action button (FAB)](/design/ui/cars/guides/components/fab): Place the most important actions on the
  screen a tap away.
* [Header](/design/ui/cars/guides/components/header): Help drivers understand which app and what part of the app
  they're using.
* [Row](/design/ui/cars/guides/components/row): Display a small amount of text, plus other options.

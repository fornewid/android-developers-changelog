# Multi-task shopping Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.




Expedite informed, confident buying decisions by enabling shoppers to view
products in one window while researching product ratings, reviews, and reports
in another.

![](/static/images/large-screens/gallery/samples/shopping/gallery_shopping_multiwindow.png)

Make purchasing quick and easy with a drag-and-drop shopping cart. Capture
prospective purchases in a drag-and-drop wish list. Enable shoppers to share
bargains and buys with family and friends using drag and drop from shopping apps
to chat or email apps in multi-window mode.

![](/static/images/large-screens/gallery/samples/shopping/gallery_shopping_dragdrop.png)

descriptive, explanatory, or other supplementary information—the item detail.

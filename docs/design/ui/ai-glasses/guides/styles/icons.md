# Icons on glasses Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.




Icons must be considered to be glanceable and discernible on glasses displays.
Use rounded icon styles with a heavier weight to prevent halation effects.

**Note:** If you don't need custom iconography, use Material Symbols Rounded.

![Design elements should be anchored to the bottom of the frame.](/static/images/design/ui/glasses/guides/glasses_style_icon_unfilled.png)


**Use unfilled** icons to avoid halation, or "light bleed".

![Design elements should be anchored to the bottom of the frame.](/static/images/design/ui/glasses/guides/glasses_style_icon_unfilled.png)


**Use a heavier stroke weight and rounded icon variants**for greater readability and harmonize with system typography, avoid thin strokes.

**Note:** For icons, check out Material Symbols. Available on [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/icons).

# Developer Podcasts

### Developer Podcasts

Keep up to date on what's new and notable in the world of Android.

## Podcast Series

![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/android-backstage.svg)  

### Android Developer Backstage

The ADB podcast is by and for Android developers. Hosted by engineers on the Android team at Google, this show covers a variety of topics via in-depth technical discussions and interviews with Android engineers.  
[open_in_newListen now](http://adbackstage.libsyn.com/)  
![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/newsletter.svg)  

### Now in Android

For developers who want to know about the latest happenings in the Andoid universe. This series covers recent platform and library releases, videos, articles, codelabs, and anything else that might be interesting for Android app developers.  
[open_in_newListen now](http://nowinandroid.googledevelopers.libsynpro.com/)  
![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/microphone-console.svg)  

### App, Games, \& Insights

For developers and product owners, the Apps, Games, \& Insights podcast brings the latest insights, stories, and discussion from industry experts.  
[Listen now](http://developer.android.com/podcasts/apps-games-insights)

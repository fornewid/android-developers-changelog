# From startup to global growth, insights from a Venture Capital expert

EP 3 \| 19 MAR 2020

### From startup to global growth, insights from a Venture Capital expert

[\< Go back to episodes](http://developer.android.com/podcasts/apps-games-insights)Whether it is kickstarting a great idea or accelerating growth from an established base, somewhere along the journey most companies will look for additional funding. For technology companies, venture capital can be the ideal solution bringing both funding and business acumen too. This episode's guest is Matteo Vallone, Principal at Cherry Ventures with advice on how to make your development business attractive to venture capitalists and what to expect after you have secured investment.

### Matteo Vallone

Matteo Vallone, currently Principal at Cherry Ventures and prior at Initial Capital, has been investing in gaming, media, and consumer services for several years. Before becoming an investor he spent more than 9 years at Google. At Google he was a Product Manager for YouTube during the years after its acquisition by Google. After that he was on the Google Play Apps and Games Business Development team, where he focused on relationships with startups and investors, and led the games category for Europe.

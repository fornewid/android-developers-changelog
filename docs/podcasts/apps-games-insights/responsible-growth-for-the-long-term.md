# Responsible growth for the long term

EP 1 \| 5 MAR 2020

### Responsible growth and building businesses for the long term; why they go hand in hand

[\< Go back to episodes](http://developer.android.com/podcasts/apps-games-insights)Going all out to maximize the revenue from your app or game won't necessarily guarantee that you'll have a healthy business in the future. Sometimes, respecting your user and pulling back from extracting everything you can from them may be the key to long-term success. A more holistic approach, one which considers the game, the players, and the people who create the game can reap big rewards. In this episode our hosts are joined by Sarah Fuchs and Judy Chen from Crowdstar at Glu mobile, a game developer that has built their long term success by taking an unconventional path by game industry standards...

### Sarah Fuchs

Sarah Fuchs is Vice President and General Manager of Covet Fashion, the world's leading fashion entertainment platform bridging real-world fashion to mobile gaming. Sarah is passionate about creating a best-in-class team with a rich diversity of thought. This team empathizes with and empowers players of Covet Fashion to express and share their style with the world. Prior to leading the Covet Fashion team at Glu Mobile, Sarah served as the Head of Production for The Sims Mobile at Electronic Arts, and held various leadership roles at EA/Maxis, DeNA, ngmoco:) and Activision. She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from George Washington University; her other full time job is being a mom.  
![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/podcasts/judy-chen-agi-ep-1.jpg)  
Director of Product \&  
Management at Design Home

### Judy Chen

Judy Chen is Director of Product Management at Design Home, the leading mobile game that lets consumers play with the world of interior design. Judy leads the team behind Design Home's product strategy, creating an empowering and innovative gaming playground for the game's over one million daily players. Prior to joining Design Home at Glu Mobile, Judy served as the co-founder and CEO at iCrypto, Inc., a Data Science Consultant at Stitch Fix, and President at Mobile High-Definition Link (MHL) a joint development by Nokia and Samsung.Judy has a PhD in Electrical Engineering and Management from Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

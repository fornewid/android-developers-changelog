# Additional developer resources for the Google Play Billing Library

## Videos

- [What's new in Play Commerce (2022)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7ky2PZl16i4)
  - Overview of new acquisition, engagement, and monetization tools
- [The "purchase anywhere" paradigm shift (Android Dev Summit '19)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L2aiHRD6Pfk) - Learn about "Purchase Anywhere". Topics include promotions, subscribe-and-install, cash purchases and pending transactions, and remote purchase approvals.
- [10 ways to increase your earnings](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=irWymeJ9uXo) - Help grow the earnings from your apps and games with these tips.
- [10 tips for launching your app](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=psu3pPdfYSM) - Make your app launch successful.
- [Driving buyer behavior with IAP and subscriptions](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_uDTVLpCsXU) - This Google Play Business Growth webinar series session focuses on monetization through in-app purchases or subscription-based business models. You'll also learn ideas for how to evaluate trends for different types of buyer behavior.
- [Acquiring and keeping new users](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0_7RYwu7zQM) - This Google Play Business Growth webinar series session focuses on user acquisition and retention.

## Blog posts and other articles

- [Implementing linkedPurchaseToken correctly to prevent duplicate subscriptions](https://medium.com/androiddevelopers/implementing-linkedpurchasetoken-correctly-to-prevent-duplicate-subscriptions-82dfbf7167da) - Learn about the `linkedPurchaseToken` field that shows you when multiple purchase tokens belong to the same subscription.

# 12L feature drop

### 12L feature drop

12L is a special feature drop that makes Android 12 even better on tablets and foldable devices. With 12L, we've optimized and polished the system UI for large screens, made multitasking more powerful and intuitive, and improved compatibility support so apps look better right out of the box. Test your apps on 12L today and optimize for large screen devices!  
[Get started](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/get)[Optimize your app](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L#optimize-for-large-screens)

## What's in 12L?

[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/about/versions/12/12L-foldable-land-shade.png)](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#system-ui)  

### [An OS optimized for large screens](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#system-ui)

In 12L, we've refined the UI to make Android more beautiful and easier to use on screens larger than  
600sp---across notifications, quick settings, lockscreen, overview, home screen, and more. Try your app with the UI updates!  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#system-ui)[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/about/versions/12/12L-multitasking.gif)](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#multitasking)  

### [Built for multitasking](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#multitasking)

We've made multitasking more powerful and intuitive. 12L includes a new taskbar on large screens that lets users drag and drop an app into split-screen mode and instantly switch apps on the fly. Make sure your apps are ready for split-screen mode!  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#multitasking)[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/about/versions/12/12L-compatibility.png)](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#compatibility)  

### [Improved compatibility experience](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#compatibility)

Visual and stability improvements to compatibility mode in 12L provide a better experience for users and help all apps look better by default. Test your apps to make sure they're optimized for large screens.  
[Learn more](http://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/12L/summary#compatibility)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/foldables.svg)

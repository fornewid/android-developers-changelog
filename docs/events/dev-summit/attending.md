# Attending

## Location

Android Dev Summit 2019 is coming to the Google Event Center (MP7) in Sunnyvale, CA on October 23-24, 2019.  
Can't attend in person? Catch the livestream on this website, or follow us on[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/androiddevelopers)!

## Attending Details

All presentations are in English and will be live captioned. Attendance at sessions and workshops is on a first-come, first-served basis.

See the[FAQ](https://developer.android.com/dev-summit/faq)for more details about attending Android Dev Summit 2019.  
-

  #### Community guidelines

  Attendees please read the[Community Guidelines](https://developer.android.com/dev-summit/community-guidelines)for the event. At Android Dev Summit, we take our[Community Guidelines](https://developer.android.com/dev-summit/community-guidelines)seriously, and all attendees, speakers, and staff at our event are required to agree to these guidelines. If you have any questions about this, please contact us at[android-dev-summit@google.com](mailto:android-dev-summit@google.com).
-

  #### Badge pick-up and breakfast

  The two-day event kicks off on Wednesday, 23 October with badge pick-up and breakfast starting at 8:00 AM and the keynote address at 10:00 AM. We recommend arriving at the Google Event Center (MP7) in time to register, enjoy the complimentary breakfast, and get comfortable before the keynote starts at 10:00 AM.

  Please bring a government-issued photo ID to register. At check-in, we will verify your photo ID prior to assigning an event badge to you. For example, government-issued licenses and passports are valid forms of identification. The name you used when you registered for the event must be exactly the same as the government-issued ID you present to us at check-in. If you don't have proper identification, you won't receive a badge and you won't be admitted into the summit. You can[update your registration information](https://events.withgoogle.com/android-dev-summit-2019/registrations/my-rsvp/confirm-account/)(including name) prior to the event.
-

  #### Meals

Attendees are offered complimentary breakfast, lunch, and snacks on both days of the conference. Food and drinks will also be available on Day 1 during the After Party event  
-

  #### Accessibility for attendees

  Android Dev Summit is meant to be inclusive and accessible to all attendees, including people with physical disabilities. We make sure that Google Event Center (MP7) is an accessible (ADA-compliant) venue, and live captioning services will be provided in the general sessions. However, we are aware that accessibility issues are diverse and this might not cover everything.

  If you have any accessibility needs, please let us know by emailing[android-dev-summit@google.com](mailto:android-dev-summit@google.com). This information will be kept private and distributed only to the individuals who need it to complete accessibility requests. Otherwise, please ask a member of staff at the help desk for assistance or questions about accessibility during the event.
-

  #### Youth attendance

  Attendees must be at least 15 years old to attend unaccompanied. All attendees under 18 years of age must return a signed parental/guardian consent form in advance of the event. You will be contacted via email with parental consent forms by October 3 or you can email us at[android-dev-summit@google.com](mailto:android-dev-summit@google.com)to obtain and submit consent forms prior to attending the event.
-

  #### Coat check

  Coat check will be provided for free for the duration of event. Located on the first floor of the venue, you can follow the signage onsite to direct you to the correct room or ask any staff member for help after receiving your badge and SWAG at registration.

### Mothers room, family lounge, and multifaith contemplation room

There will be a multifaith contemplation room and a mothers room available on site throughout the event. Also, there will be a family lounge where families can sit together to watch the livestream. Please ask the registration staff for information when you check in.  

### Childcare

We do not offer childcare on site. However, we're excited to offer child-care reimbursement for Android Dev Summit 2019! Children ages 12 years or younger are eligible. Please email us at[android-dev-summit@google.com](mailto:android-dev-summit@google.com)by 18 October. Due to limited availability, we might not be able to assist with all requests. To receive reimbursement, a completed W9, final invoice for reimbursement with supporting receipts must be submitted no later than November 22.

# Why you are seeing recommendations on developer.android.com

Page recommendations on developer.android.com help you find related and popular content as you browse. We generate recommendations by analyzing relationships among pages and/or collecting the information below.

## Information collected to generate recommendations

- Your info:
  - Your information listed in your[developer profile](https://developers.google.com/profile/u/me)
- Your activity:
  - Pages you have visited in your previous sessions on developer.android.com only
  - Pages you have visited in your current session on developer.android.com only
  - Specific projects or product areas you have repeatedly searched on developer.android.com only
- Other info:
  - Timestamp of page visit

## Manage your recommendations

Recommendations for developer.android.com are dependent on you Web \& App Activity in[My Activity](http://myactivity.google.com). To review what information is collected in your Web \& App Activity and if you would like to enable or disable it, please visit your[My Activity](http://myactivity.google.com).

If Web \& App Activity is disabled, you may still see content recommendations on developer.android.com. These recommendations are generic and based only on the current page. No user-specific information is collected or used to provide these recommendations.

**Note:** Any information collected will be used in accordance with[Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

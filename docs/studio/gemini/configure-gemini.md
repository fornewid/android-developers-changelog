# Configure Gemini

To access Gemini in Android Studio's settings, click on the Gemini icon at the bottom of the Studio IDE![](https://developer.android.com/static/studio/gemini/images/gemini-active.png)and select**Configure Gemini** . You can also quickly toggle the[code completion](https://developer.android.com/studio/gemini/code-completion)feature on and off right there in the menu.
![](https://developer.android.com/static/studio/gemini/images/gemini-quick-settings.png)

Alternatively, access Gemini's settings through**File** (**Android Studio** on macOS)**\> Settings \> Tools \> Gemini**.
![](https://developer.android.com/static/studio/gemini/images/gemini-settings.png)

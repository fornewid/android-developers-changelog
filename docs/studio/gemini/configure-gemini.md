# Configure Gemini Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.




To access Gemini in Android Studio's settings, click on the Gemini icon at
the bottom of the Studio IDE ![](/static/studio/gemini/images/gemini-active.png)
and select **Configure Gemini**. You can also quickly toggle the
[code completion](/studio/gemini/code-completion) feature on and off right there
in the menu.

![](/static/studio/gemini/images/gemini-quick-settings.png)

Alternatively, access Gemini's settings through **File** (**Android Studio** on
macOS) **> Settings > Tools > AI**.

![](/static/studio/gemini/images/gemini-settings.png)

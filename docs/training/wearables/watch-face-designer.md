# Customize watch face appearance using Watch Face Designer

The guides on the following pages describe how to use the[Watch Face Designer plugin](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1537485193225852308/watch-face-designer), available from our Figma Community page.
| **Note:** Watch Face Designer contains components from[open source packages used under their respective licenses](https://developer.android.com/training/wearables/watch-face-designer/licenses).

- **[Basics](https://developer.android.com/training/wearables/watch-face-designer/basics):**Learn how to create watch faces, add time readouts, and more.
- **[Advanced usage](https://developer.android.com/training/wearables/watch-face-designer/advanced):**Add subdials, complications, and color themes.
- **[Export options](https://developer.android.com/training/wearables/watch-face-designer/export):**Continue your work in Android Studio, save as an APK or AAB file, or use a one-click deploy workflow.
- **[Publishing to Google Play](https://developer.android.com/training/wearables/watch-face-designer/publish):**Use the Google Play Console to publish your watch face from Watch Face Designer.

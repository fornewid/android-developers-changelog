## What's in Wear OS 4

![](https://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/lightning.svg)

### Power optimizations

Wear OS 4 introduces several updates and optimizations to help your app run as smoothly and efficiently as possible.

![](https://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/wear-os-development.svg)

### Watch Face Format

Bring your creative vision to life using either Watch Face Studio or the Watch Face Format.

![](https://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/health-services-logo.svg)

### Health Services improvements

Health Services offers improved permissions for background body sensors. On the emulator, test a wider variety of exercise types more seamlessly.

![](https://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/code-2.svg)

### 64-bit kernel and user space

Prepare your app for 64-bit only hardware and take advantage of the performance and security improvements introduced in Wear OS 4.

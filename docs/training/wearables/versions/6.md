## Capabilities to explore in Wear OS 6

palette  

### Embrace expressive design

The latest version of Wear OS supports a design refresh using Material 3 Expressive components. They let you offer more personal customization across your app's surfaces.  
watch  

### Watch face enhancements

Watch Face Push supports marketplace experience for your watch faces. Expand upon your existing watch faces with a new version of Watch Face Format, which includes enhancements such as a photo selection experience.  
schedule  

### A more consistent always-on experience

Wear OS 6 provides a consistent always-on display experience across devices, which remembers and shows the previous top activity.

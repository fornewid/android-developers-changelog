# Get started with Wear OS Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.



Wear OS helps users stay connected, achieve their health and fitness goals, and
express their personal style.

As an Android developer, you're on familiar ground. Wear OS embraces the best
practices of [Modern Android Development](/modern-android-development), allowing you to apply your
knowledge of its core libraries, principles, and techniques. For example, when
it comes to building your user interface, we recommend [Compose for Wear OS](/training/wearables/compose).
As a cornerstone of the modern toolkit, it helps you write beautiful, efficient
apps with less code.

Whether you want to start with high-level design concepts or jump straight into
building your first app, these guides will set you on the right path.

## Get started

* **[Design your Wear OS user interface ⍈](/design/ui/wear):** The design hub for Wear OS,
  covering foundations, components, and best practices.
* **[Principles of Wear OS development](/training/wearables/principles):** Principles behind high-quality
  Wear OS apps, like designing for glanceability and offline use.
* **[Wear OS pathway](/courses/pathways/wear):** A guided learning path with tutorials and codelabs
  to build your skills sequentially.
* **[Wear OS user interfaces](/training/wearables/user-interfaces):** An overview of Wear OS UI surfaces (apps,
  tiles, complications) and when to use each one.
* **[Use Jetpack Compose on Wear OS](/training/wearables/compose):** A guide to setting up your project
  and building UIs with Compose for Wear OS, our recommended toolkit.
* **[Create and run a wearable app](/training/wearables/get-started/creating):** A step-by-step tutorial for creating
  and running your first app on an emulator or physical device.
* **[Debug a Wear OS app](/training/wearables/get-started/debugging):** Instructions for connecting a physical watch
  to your computer for debugging over Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or USB.
* **[Wear OS app quality](/docs/quality-guidelines/wear-app-quality):** The official checklist of quality guidelines
  for publishing a Wear OS app on Google Play.

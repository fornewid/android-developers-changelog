# Android 5G

![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/android-5g/logo-android-5g.svg)  

### Android 5G

Take your app to the next level and redefine the user experience by incorporating 5G capabilities.

*** ** * ** ***

### Transforming your app with 5G

Indoor \> Outdoor

### [Turn indoor use cases into outdoor use cases](http://developer.android.com/training/connectivity/5g/enhance-with-5g#indoor-outdoor)

Video chat on the go. Download what you need when you need it. Play multi-player games wherever you happen to be.  
Photo AR

### [Turn photo-centric UX into video-centric or AR-centric UX](http://developer.android.com/training/connectivity/5g/enhance-with-5g#photo-ar)

Upgrade your users' experience by replacing photos with videos and by incorporating augmented reality into your UX.  
Prefetch

### [Prefetch helpfully: delight rather than buffer](http://developer.android.com/training/connectivity/5g/enhance-with-5g#prefetch)

Use the increased bandwidth to prefetch content, further enhancing the responsiveness of your app.  
Niche

### [Turn niche use cases into mainstream use cases](http://developer.android.com/training/connectivity/5g/enhance-with-5g#niche)

Streaming content on older networks was the exception. Make streaming content the new mainstream with 5G.

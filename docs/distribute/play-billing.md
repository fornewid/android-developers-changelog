# Google Play's billing system

![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/hero-illustrations/play-billing-hero.svg)  

### Google Play's
billing system

Sell digital in-app products and subscriptions in your app. Get started with Google Play's billing system to leverage Google Play Commerce -- our monetization tools, features, and programs that are deeply integrated into Google Play to support your monetization goals.  
[Learn More](http://developer.android.com/google/play/billing)

## Integrate with Google Play's
billing system

[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/sync.svg)](http://developer.android.com/google/play/billing/integrate)  

### [Connect and Integrate your app](http://developer.android.com/google/play/billing/integrate)

Learn how to add the Google Play Billing Library to your app and connect your app and backend to Google Play's billing system.  
[Get started](http://developer.android.com/google/play/billing/integrate)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/distribute/images/play-billing-logo.svg)](http://developer.android.com/reference/com/android/billingclient/classes)  

### [Google Play Billing Library](http://developer.android.com/reference/com/android/billingclient/classes)

This library provides a straightforward and simple interface for sending in-app billing requests and managing in-app billing transactions.  
[See the API reference](http://developer.android.com/reference/com/android/billingclient/classes)

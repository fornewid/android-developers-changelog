# Play Points overview

The Google Play Points program rewards engaged members and benefits developers by increasing user retention and reinvestment.[Google Play Points](https://play.google.com/console/about/googleplaypoints/)members can exchange accrued points for discounted coupons and in-app products offered as promotions by developers.

Participation in Play Points is available to select developers. To apply, submit the Google Play Points[interest form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScLZRRwurcI7ukTNoDZY9BuAiEndCpffSTW3KFYoAp_xoZgbw/viewform?_ga=2.171609827.2141389536.1634151076-501269176.1628196488&_gac=1.124381816.1630528233.CjwKCAjwybyJBhBwEiwAvz4G7-aYMjxOtGdcfeRQu0guaw6r2ETwZx6SF7YHXF-BmpNvdS_cGdqemxoCJfoQAvD_BwE).

## Get started

**For in-app products:**

To offer your in-app products as Play Points promotions and ensure the best user experience, you must complete the following tasks:

1. [Create](https://developer.android.com/guide/playpoints/create-products)in-app products and Play Points promotions.
2. [Detect](https://developer.android.com/guide/playpoints/deliver-items)and deliver in-app products.
3. [Test](https://developer.android.com/guide/playpoints/test)in-app products using promo codes.

**For one-time products:**

To offer your one-time products as Play Points promotions and ensure the best user experience, you must complete the following tasks:

1. [Create](https://developer.android.com/guide/playpoints/create-products-one-time)one-time products and Play Points promotions.
2. [Detect](https://developer.android.com/guide/playpoints/deliver-items-one-time)and deliver one-time products.
3. [Test](https://developer.android.com/guide/playpoints/test-one-time)one-time products using promo codes.

TIER 1 — Adaptive differentiated

**Objective:** Make your app [adaptive differentiated](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-1) by meeting the
[Offline\_Support](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#Offline_Support) requirement of the [Adaptive app quality guidelines](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality).

Reliable offline functionality ensures productivity regardless of network
availability.

Your app should enable offline functionality for the full or partial app feature
set with graceful degradation of capabilities for reduced or lost connectivity.

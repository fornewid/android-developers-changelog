TIER 1 — Adaptive differentiated

**Objective:** Make your app [adaptive differentiated](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-1) by meeting the following user experience requirements of the [Adaptive app quality guidelines](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality):

* [Scrollbar\_Display](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#Scrollbar_Display)
* [Hover\_Parity](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#Hover_Parity)
* [Desktop\_Menus](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#Desktop_Menus)
* [UI\_Config](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#UI_Config)
* [Request\_Fullscreen\_Mode](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#Request_Fullscreen_Mode)

Bridge the gap between mobile convenience and desktop power by differentiating
your app with the following:

* Adaptive, user-configurable layouts, including collapsible, reconfigurable
  panels
* A scrollbar that's displayed while content is being scrolled by a mouse or
  trackpad
* UI elements that display additional content, such as previews and tooltips,
  on mouse or trackpad hover
* Desktop-style menus and context menus
* Fullscreen mode

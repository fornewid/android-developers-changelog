TIER 1 — Adaptive differentiated

**Objective:** Make your app [adaptive differentiated](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-1) by meeting the following stylus requirements of the [Adaptive app quality guidelines](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality):

* [Stylus\_Draw\_Write](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/camera-audio#Stylus_Draw_Write)
* [Stylus\_Drag\_Drop](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/camera-audio#Stylus_Drag_Drop)
* [Stylus\_Enhanced](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/camera-audio#Stylus_Enhanced)

Apps differentiated for large screen devices support drawing, writing, and
erasing with a stylus. Users can drag and drop content within an app and between
apps (in multi‑window mode) using a stylus. Apps support stylus input in
note‑taking apps.

Differentiated apps support advanced stylus features, including:

* Low latency and motion prediction for real-time responsiveness
* Pressure sensitivity for variable-width strokes
* Tilt detection for shading
* Palm and finger rejection to handle inadvertent screen touches

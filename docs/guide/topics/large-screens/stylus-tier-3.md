# Stylus Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.




![Tier 3 adaptive ready icon](/static/images/docs/quality-guidelines/tier-3/tier_3_icon.png)

TIER 3 — Adaptive ready

**Objective:** Make your app [adaptive ready](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-3) by meeting the [Stylus\_Basic](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-3#Stylus_Basic) and
[Stylus\_Text\_Input](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-3#Stylus_Text_Input) requirements of the [Adaptive app quality guidelines](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality).

Productivity and creativity apps on large screen devices are at their best when
the apps support stylus input.

Enable your users to take advantage of the natural, intuitive interactivity a
stylus provides for:

* Writing and editing text
* Scrolling through lists, pickers, and other scrollable content

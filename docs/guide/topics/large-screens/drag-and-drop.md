# Drag and drop Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.



![Tier 1 adaptive differentiated icon](/static/images/docs/quality-guidelines/tier-1/tier_1_icon.png)

TIER 1 — Adaptive differentiated

**Objective:** Make your app [adaptive differentiated](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-1) by meeting the
[Drag\_Drop\_Support](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#Drag_Drop_Support) requirement of the [Adaptive app quality guidelines](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality).

Differentiate your app on large screens by supporting drag and drop within the
app and, in multi‑window mode, between apps.

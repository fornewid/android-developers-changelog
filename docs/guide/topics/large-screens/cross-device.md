TIER 1 — Adaptive differentiated

**Objective:** Make your app [adaptive differentiated](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-1) by meeting the
[Cross\_Device\_Handoff](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/desktop#Cross_Device_Handoff) requirement of the [Adaptive app quality guidelines](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality).

Apps that work on multiple types of Android devices allow users to work across
their ecosystem with a near‑equivalent state for the same task.

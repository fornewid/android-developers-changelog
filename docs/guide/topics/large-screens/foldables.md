# Foldables Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.



![Tier 1 adaptive differentiated icon](/static/images/docs/quality-guidelines/tier-1/tier_1_icon.png)

TIER 1 — Adaptive differentiated

**Objective:** Make your app [adaptive differentiated](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/tier-1) by meeting the
[Foldables\_Postures](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/foldables#Foldables_Postures) and [Foldables\_Camera](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality/experiences/foldables#Foldables_Camera) requirements of the [Adaptive
app quality guidelines](/docs/quality-guidelines/adaptive-app-quality).

Foldable postures and states such as tabletop and [`HALF_OPENED`](/reference/kotlin/androidx/window/layout/FoldingFeature.State#HALF_OPENED()) invite app
innovation. A camera preview that supports both large and small screens of a
foldable device provides an optimal user experience.

Differentiate your app on premium foldable devices with Jetpack WindowManager
APIs such as [`FoldingFeature`](/reference/kotlin/androidx/window/layout/FoldingFeature).

# Extend your app's reach to the entire Android device ecosystem Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.



This section digs into how to prepare your app for multidevice experiences
by extending your app to support more devices like tablets, foldables, and Wear
OS, voice assistants like [Google Assistant](/media/implement/assistant), and
[Cast-enabled](https://developers.google.com/cast/docs/overview) devices.

If you're new to media development on Android, consider starting instead
with the developer guides for [playback](/media/implement/playback-app) and
[editing](/media/implement/editing-app) for guidance about using the Jetpack
Media3 library to implement playback and editing features.

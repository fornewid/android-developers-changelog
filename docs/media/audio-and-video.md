# Audio and video overview Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.



This section covers the core APIs for handling audio and video data, including
use-cases like playback, editing, and recording. You'll also find
recommendations on app architecture and descriptions of platform behaviors here.

**Tip:** Check out the [Media Developer Center](/media) for best practices and guidance
on creating a best-in-class multidevice app.

## Jetpack Media3

In most cases, Media3 is our recommended solution for building audio and video
experiences. Start here if you're new to developing media features.

[Go to About Jetpack Media3](/guide/topics/media/media3)

Meet the Jetpack Media3 library and key APIs for implementing playback
use-cases.

[Go to Media3 MediaSession](/guide/topics/media/session/mediasession)

### Media3 ExoPlayer

Dive deep into `ExoPlayer`, a feature rich and extensible media player API
included in Media3.

[Go to Media3 ExoPlayer](/guide/topics/media/exoplayer)

### Media3 Cast

Extend your media app with the `Cast` module from Media3, enabling
both local and remote playback.

[Go to Media3 Cast](/media/media3/cast)

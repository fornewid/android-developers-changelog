# Google Assistant

## Google Assistant for Android Developers

Unlock user engagement for your Android applications by enabling hands-free usage and proactive assistance to increase discovery, engagement and retention of your applications across your users primary and companion devices.  
[Build with App Actions](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/assistant/overview)

## Integrate into a more helpful Google

### Create natural, conversational interactions with your users

With App Actions and Built-in Intents, you can easily enable your users to interact with your app by using their voice--all without you having to bring in a conversation design agency or build and manage your own voice tech stack.  

### Embed your app across the Android ecosystem

Phones, cars and watches each put your user in a different context or situation. With Google Assistant, you can create delightful user experiences across the Android ecosystem, from glanceable app views to deep linking directly into your app from a voice query.  

### Leverage familiar Android developer tools

Use familiar Android development tooling such as app widgets, shortcuts.xml and Android Studio. With the Assistant plugin to create a preview of your App Actions before deploying the production version of your app to the Google Play Console.[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/spot-icons/interface.svg)](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/assistant/widgets)

## Integrate your app with Assistant-enabled Android devices

![](http://developer.android.com/static/assistant/images/app-mobile.png)  

### Bring Google Assistant powered query fulfillment to your Android app

As users gravitate to faster and more natural ways to get things done on their phones, ensure your app is eligible for query fulfillment by surfacing your app's intents via App Actions.  
[App Actions for Mobile](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/assistant/get-started)![](http://developer.android.com/static/assistant/images/app-actions-wear.png)  

### Enable voice commands for your Wear apps

Enable quick and glanceable help for your users on-the-go. With App Actions for Wear, you can make it even easier for users to engage with your Wear app via voice.  
[App Actions for Wear](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/assistant/wear)![](http://developer.android.com/static/assistant/images/app-actions-cars.png)  

### Extend your Android car app

Help your users get things done in a simple, seamless, and hands-free way by integrating Google Assistant with car apps developed with Android Auto or Android Automotive OS.  
[App Actions for Cars](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/assistant/cars)![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/picto-icons/learning-resources.svg)

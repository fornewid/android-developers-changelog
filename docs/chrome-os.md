### [ChromeOS devices](http://developer.android.com/chrome-os/intro)

ChromeOS is the speedy, simple, and secure operating system that powers every Chromebook. You can distribute your Android apps to Google ChromeOS devices through the Google Play app store.  
[Get started](http://developer.android.com/chrome-os/intro) [![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/cluster-illustrations/material-design-16-9.svg)](https://material.io/)  

### [Design](https://material.io/)

Android apps connect experiences from mobile to laptop form factors. Delight your users with more ways to be productive on large screens and with keyboard, trackpad, and mouse inputs.  
[Design for ChromeOS](https://material.io/) [![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/cluster-illustrations/build-apps.svg)](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/chromeos/learn)  
ChromeOS basics

### [Build a ChromeOS app](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/chromeos/learn)

You have an existing Android app designed for phones or tablets that you want to optimize for Chromebooks. This is where you get started with Android development for ChromeOS.  
[Get started](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/chromeos/learn)  
[![](http://developer.android.com/static/images/cluster-illustrations/new-features.svg)](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/chromeos/learn/optimizing)  
ChromeOS basics

### [Optimize a ChromeOS app](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/chromeos/learn/optimizing)

Android apps have an important role to play in redefining what modern computing looks like on large screens. Just porting a mobile app to Chromebooks doesn't give your users the best experience. Learn some ways that you can tailor your experience towards laptop and convertible form factors.  
[Optimize your app for ChromeOS](http://developer.android.com/develop/devices/chromeos/learn/optimizing)

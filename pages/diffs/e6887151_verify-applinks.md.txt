+## Digital Asset Links update latency
+
+When you update the `assetlinks.json` file on your web server, the time it takes
+for these changes to be reflected on end-user devices depends on the OS version:
+
+- **Android 15 (API level 35) and higher:** The system periodically re-verifies domains in the background. Changes can take up to seven days to propagate to all end-user devices due to the system's caching and scheduled re-verification.
+- **Android 14 (API level 34) and lower:** The system does not perform periodic background re-verification. Updates to the file are typically only picked up when the app is installed or updated.
+
+**Testing updates**
+
+To force a re-verification on a specific device during testing, you can
+uninstall and reinstall the app. However, be aware that server-side caches might
+still delay the delivery of the updated `assetlinks.json` file to the device,
+even after reinstalling. Because the cache is time-based, the delay typically
+resolves automatically if you try again after a few hours.

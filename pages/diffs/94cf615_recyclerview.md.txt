-  [`ViewCompat.setOnApplyWindowInsetsListener`](/reference/androidx/core/view/ViewCompat#setOnApplyWindowInsetsListener(android.view.View,androidx.core.view.OnApplyWindowInsetsListener)).
-* Allow the list items to draw under the system bars while scrolling by setting
-  [`android:clipToPadding`](/reference/android/view/ViewGroup#attr_android:clipToPadding)
-  to `false` on the `RecyclerView`.

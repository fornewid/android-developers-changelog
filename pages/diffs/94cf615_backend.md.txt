 Your secure server backend plays a key role in your app's management of in-app
 purchases through Google Play. Google Play's billing system provides a way to
 manage the most important aspects of your digital product business, from setting
 up the catalog to tracking your transactions.

 functionality related to your digital product sales on Google Play.

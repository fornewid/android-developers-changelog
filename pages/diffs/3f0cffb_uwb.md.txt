 ## Steps

-   support UWB using

-   such as [`BluetoothGatt`](/reference/android/bluetooth/BluetoothGatt).

 ## Usage restrictions
 
 The following restrictions apply to usage of the UWB API:
 
-1. The app initiating new UWB ranging sessions must be a foreground
-   app or service unless background ranging is supported as illustrated
-   earlier.
-2. When the app moves to background (while the session is ongoing), the app
-   may no longer receive ranging reports. The UWB session will however
-   continue to be maintained in the lower layers. When the app moves back to the
-   foreground, the ranging reports will resume.
+1. The app initiating new UWB ranging sessions must be a foreground app or service unless background ranging is supported as illustrated earlier.
+2. When the app moves to background (while the session is ongoing), the app may no longer receive ranging reports. The UWB session will however continue to be maintained in the lower layers. When the app moves back to the foreground, the ranging reports will resume.

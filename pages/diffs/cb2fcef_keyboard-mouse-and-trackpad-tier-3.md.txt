 Large screen devices often include a keyboard, mouse, or trackpad for enhanced
 user input and interactivity.
 
 Enable your app to support:
 
-* Input from an external keyboard
-* Context switching between physical and virtual keyboards
-* Basic mouse and trackpad interactions, such as click, select, and scroll

 #### The minimum size for text is 0.6°
 
-Most text should be between 0.7° - 0.9° Based on legibility research, the
-minimum size for readable body text is around 0.6° at any given depth. To meet
-the minimum legibility metric, text must be at least 18px in size. 30 PPD × 0.6°
-minimum = 18px text size.

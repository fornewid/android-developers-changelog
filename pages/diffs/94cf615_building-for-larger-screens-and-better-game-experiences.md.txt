+The growth of Android and its application beyond smartphones means that apps and games are no longer constrained by the dimensions of a single pocketable screen: they can now run on devices that offer traditional laptop and desktop screen sizes. At the same time, in order to continue to engage with players regardless of the gaming platform, mature mobile game franchises are looking for opportunities beyond mobile for this.
 
 This episode's guest is Maximiliano Rodriguez, Platforms Operations Director at Gameloft who discusses the challenges in taking games to larger screens or new platforms.

 ### Maximiliano Rodriguez
 
 Maximiliano Rodriguez is Platforms Operations Director at Gameloft. With a major in Graphic Design and Communication, he has spent the last 15 years in the game industry in various roles throughout LATAM, APAC, and Europe. He has helped adapt services to take advantage of technological advances, from hardware to platforms and ecosystems. Originally from Buenos Aires, Argentina, he is a big fan of football and a good glass of Malbec.
